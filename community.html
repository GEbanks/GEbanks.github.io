Community Library & Writing Center (Patio de Letras)

A quiet courtyard library/writing area where people can study, read books, and write their own stories. Open late inot the night with free Wi-Fi and shaded outdoor tables.

Community Property: Parks & Commerce

Río Verde Park

A shady riverwalk with concrete paths, a fishing dock, a small bridge, and native plants that keep the river clean and beutiful.

The Ebanks Plaza

Street front stalls for fresh fruit, panadería, and artisans. Parking and other vehicles are behind buildings to keep the plaza safe and easy to access for pedestrians .

Cleaning Crews

Groups of volunteers meet weekly to pick up trash, maintain trails, and plant native species to keep our community beautiful.
