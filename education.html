Schools & Learning

Traditional K‑12

  • STEM labs, art studios, and music rooms.
  • Block scheduling for internships, sports, or work.
  • Free breakfast/lunch and late buses for clubs.

Non Traditional Options

  • Career & Tech campus (IT, culinary, health, electrical, welding).
  • Community Learning Patio open nights/weekends for GED, English, and coding classes.
  • Paid internships with businesses in the Plaza and around the community.
